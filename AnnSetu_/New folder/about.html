About Us

Our Mission

To create a hunger-free and waste-free India by making food donation simple and accessible. We connect surplus food from donors to those in need, ensuring no edible food goes to waste while feeding communities in a sustainable way.

Learn More

Impact

Our Vision

To create a future where no one sleeps hungry, and no food goes to waste. 40% of food in India is wasted while millions go hungry, and our platform ensures every saved meal feeds someone in need. We envision a sustainable system where surplus food is efficiently redistributed, ensuring every meal serves a purpose and contributes to a healthier, more nourished society.
