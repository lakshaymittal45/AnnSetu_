AnnSetu - Mission

Our Mission

India produces 450 million tons of food annually, yet 40% of it (68 million tons) goes to waste. At the same time,over 195 million Indians (14% of the population) suffer from malnutrition and food insecurity. This mismatch between abundance and scarcity is what AnnSetu aims to solve.

Our mission is to create an efficient, technology-driven food redistribution networkthat connects:

  • Restaurants, hotels, catering businesses, and event planners with NGOs, shelters, and food banks.
  • Food donors with verified logistics providers and volunteers for real-time food collection.
  • Corporates and government initiatives with surplus food management programs.

By streamlining surplus food redistribution, AnnSetu ensures that fresh, nutritious meals reach the underprivileged instead of ending up in landfills.
